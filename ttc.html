Ashtanga Yoga with Sheshadri and Harish

TTC

Ashtanga vinyasa yoga teacher training course is an intensive and in-depth course that consists of 3 weeks.

Practitioners can understand the importance of various asana sequencing practice. Understand the techniques of breathing while practising asana and pranayama to bring out the positive energy. assured to develop yogic skills to handle varied levels of practitioners in different ways.

    Course consists of:
  1. Ashtanga Yoga ( Mysore style / Led class )
  2. Pranayama [Basic and Advance]
  3. Teacher Training Course [TTC]
  4. Certification Course
  5. Mudra's and Bandha's
  6. Yoga Sutra Chanting
  7. Shat Kriyas
  8. Cooking class

Certificate:

TTC certificate will be issued to the student who has successfully complete the course.
