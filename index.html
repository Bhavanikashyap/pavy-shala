PAVY Shala | Ashtanga Yoga with Sheshadri and Harish is an Institute to teach Ashtanga Vinyasa Yoga in Mysuru. Sheshadri - the Founder of PAVY Shala is belongs to the most experienced, world renowned and certified teacher in Ashtanga Vinyasa Yoga. Harish - the director of PAVY Shala is the experienced, world renowned and certified teacher in Ashtanga Vinyasa Yoga.

Sheshadri and his son Harish will give maximum personal attention to every practitioner and convince the students giving useful advises and helpful adjustments and allow his students deeper experience of the various Asanas and Pranayama to develop and deepen the practice in an sensitive way of each student on their individual capacity.

Our aim is to teach traditional mysuru ashtanga vinyasa yogasana and pranayama for both beginners and advance yoga practitioners.
