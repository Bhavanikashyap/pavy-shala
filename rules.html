PAVY Shala Rules and Regulations

  • Students who like to register yoga courses should register a day earlier and bring copies of your passport, visa and 2 passport size photographs.
  • Students should fill the registration form at the shala itself when they arrive.
  • Students must pay by cash when they register at shala and full fees are expected from the start date.
  • Course fees paid at the time of registration is non refundable or not transferable for future classes.
  • Please Register your name a day earlier for Drop-in Class.
  • Please inform your teachers of any injuries or medical issues you may have before the class begins.
  • Students should arrive 10 minutes early so that classes can start on time.
  • Sundays and Moon days are holiday.
  • It is recommended to practice for one month, but it is possible to attend classes for less than one month depending on your schedule(1 week or 2 weeks). However, you can begin the course at any time.
  • Kindly bring your personal yoga mat for practice.
  • Switch off your mobile phones while entering the Shala. Photography/Videography is strictly prohibited.
  • PAVY Shala does not provide food, accommodation and transport to yoga students. You must have to arrange it by yourself.
  • PAVY Shala and its management are not responsible for any incidents occurring outside the Shala premises.
  • The Shala is a sacred space for spiritual learning and worship. Please maintain its decorum.
